• You can create or edit Developer Groups here.
  • Each group must have one Product Owner, one Kanban Master and at least one developer.
  • All these roles must be correctly represented when the Developer group is formed or edited.
  • Only users that are qualified for a certain role can be chosen to act in that role.
  • Product Owner is the person representing the interests of the buyer.
  • Kanban Master is the person responsible for enforcing and regulating Kanban methodology during development.
  • Developer is the person who is working on developing the project according to the Kanban methodology.
